About Sereen

#feelSereen

Sereen is a peer-to-peer support network for musicians with mental health challenges and concerns.

Our goal is to revolutionize how musicians fight their mental health in an accessible and affordable way while bringing forth a fresh experience.

Let's harmonize mental wellness in music. Sign up to our beta waitlist!

How It Works

3 Easy Steps

Go through the onboarding process and make an account
