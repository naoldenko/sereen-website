What is Sereen?

Musicians in the music industry contend with severe pressures, unpredictable lifestyles, isolation, criticism, and a higher risk of substance abuse, often lacking access to mental health resources.

We aim to tackle these challenges by creating an anonymous mental health community app tailored to musicians, offering a safe space for sharing, robust moderation, and connections to mental health support.

Our goal is to foster a supportive environment that acknowledges their unique struggles and provides discreet access to the help they need.

How does it work?

Sereen will have multiple subcommunities based off mental health challenges/concerns that people have, in which they can discuss and reply anonymously.

We employ advanced sentiment analysis to promptly remove any offensive content. Our clear guidelines and reporting system guarantee a respectful and secure environment. Our unwavering dedication to professionalism ensures a safe and welcoming space for musicians to discuss mental health.

In addition to being a peer-to-peer support network, Sereen provides an innovative gamification element, offering musicians incentives for their active engagement within the app. As you progress through levels, you'll not only gain personal growth but also have the opportunity to get paid for assisting others.

Your valuable time spent on Sereen becomes an enriching experience where support and collaboration lead to mutual benefits. Join us in this exciting journey of self-improvement and community support, all while unlocking rewarding opportunities along the way.
